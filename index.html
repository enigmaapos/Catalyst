Catalyst NFT Staking Protocol

Welcome to the Catalyst NFT Staking Protocol. This system allows users to stake NFTs to earn CATA rewards and participate in governance.

Show Transparency

The Catalyst NFT Staking Protocol is a full system with a two-tier collection structure (Verified and Unverified), governance capabilities based on burned CATA tokens, and dynamic fees. It includes anti-flash protections and a 20,000 stake cap per collection. [cite_start]The system also features a Burner Bonus for the top 1% of token burners, paid from the treasury[cite: 268].

[cite_start]The protocol's smart contract, named CatalystNFTStaking, inherits from several OpenZeppelin contracts for secure access control, reentrancy protection, and pausing functionality[cite: 270]. [cite_start]All actions within the protocol are publicly recorded via events, such as NFTStaked, ProposalCreated, and BurnerBonusDistributed[cite: 309, 311, 314].

Proposals

[cite_start]

Proposals can be created by users who meet certain eligibility criteria, such as being a top collection staker or having a minimum CATA burn contribution[cite: 431, 435]. [cite_start]Voting is based on a scaled weight system, with a per-collection vote cap to prevent a single collection from dominating the process[cite: 398].
